Dynasty Archer

About

Dynasty Archers is a journey through the historical battles of the Three Kingdoms era, where you will overcome challenges and perils with an extraordinary set of skills and weapons. Starting as a newcomer, you will be trained to master skills and become an unparalleled military commander, facing down armies and epic bosses alone.

Introduction

This project was a comprehensive overhaul of a pre-existing title, with the core mission of transforming it with a new thematic identity and significantly enhanced gameplay systems to boost long-term player retention. My role was central to this evolution, spanning both technical engineering and creative design.

A primary technical challenge was delivering high-fidelity visuals and complex, multi-layered gameplay on a wide range of mobile devices. The new, visually rich VFX for character skills caused significant performance drops during intense combat scenes. Instead of constantly instantiating and destroying particle effects—a costly operation that causes garbage collection spikes and stuttering—I engineered a robust object pooling system. This resulted in a dramatically smoother gameplay experience, maintaining a stable 60 FPS even when hundreds of particles were on-screen.

From a design perspective, my focus was on architecting a scalable character skill system that allowed for deep strategic variety. I designed and balanced the core mechanics, stats, and synergistic interactions for all new character skills and overhauled the AI for mobs and bosses, implementing varied attack patterns and decision-making heuristics to create more dynamic and challenging encounters. This project taught me the critical importance of performance profiling early and often, solidifying a "performance-by-design" mindset that I now apply to all my work.
