Dynasty Archer

About

Dynasty Archers is a journey through the historical battles of the Three Kingdoms era, where you will overcome challenges and perils with an extraordinary set of skills and weapons. Starting as a newcomer, you will be trained to master skills and become an unparalleled military commander, facing down armies and epic bosses alone.

Project Info

  • Role: Game Developer & Designer
  • Team Size: 5-10
  • Time frame: 2022 - Present
  • Engine: Unity

Development Deep Dive

This project was a comprehensive overhaul of a pre-existing title, with the core mission of transforming it with a new thematic identity and significantly enhanced gameplay systems to boost long-term player retention. My role was central to this evolution, spanning the full spectrum of both technical engineering and creative design.

From a design perspective, my focus was on architecting a scalable character skill system that allowed for deep strategic variety. I designed and balanced the core mechanics, stats, and synergistic interactions for all new character skills. A significant part of this process involved overhauling the AI for all mobs and bosses, implementing varied attack patterns, movement logic, and decision-making heuristics to create more dynamic, unpredictable, and challenging encounters. I also meticulously balanced the in-game economy by tuning resource drops and difficulty curves across multiple chapters to ensure a fair yet rewarding player progression.

Technically, a primary challenge was delivering high-fidelity visuals on a wide range of mobile devices. The new, visually rich VFX for character skills caused significant performance drops during intense combat scenes. Instead of constantly instantiating and destroying particle effects—a costly operation that causes garbage collection spikes and stuttering—I engineered a robust object pooling system. This resulted in a dramatically smoother gameplay experience, maintaining a stable 60 FPS even when hundreds of particles were on-screen. This project taught me the critical importance of performance profiling early and often, solidifying a "performance-by-design" mindset that I now apply to all my work.

My Contributions

As a hybrid developer and designer, I was responsible for both re-engineering core systems and shaping the player experience.

As a Game Developer:
  • Codebase Refactoring & Optimization: Took ownership of the original codebase to perform a significant reskin and refactor, which involved optimizing critical game loops, reducing memory allocation, and restructuring code for better scalability.
  • Advanced AI Behavior: Enhanced the AI for both standard mobs and bosses by implementing a state machine, allowing enemies to have varied attack patterns and react dynamically to player positions.
  • VFX Integration: Created and integrated custom Visual Effects (VFX) for all new character skills using Unity's particle system, ensuring they were both visually impactful and performance-friendly.
  • UI Implementation & Logic: Developed the complete user interface from design mockups, writing C# scripts to manage all UI functionalities, connect UI elements to backend game systems, and ensure responsiveness across diverse screen sizes.
As a Game Designer:
  • Skill System Design: Designed and balanced the entire skill system for over 10 unique playable characters, creating distinct gameplay mechanics and defining a clear progression path for each.
  • Gameplay & Combat Balancing: Meticulously fine-tuned combat parameters, including player damage, enemy health scaling, and skill cooldowns to create a challenging yet rewarding experience.
  • User Experience Enhancement: Analyzed player feedback to identify pain points and subsequently designed key improvements, such as clearer visual cues for enemy attacks and a more intuitive UI for skill selection.

Heroes Survivor

About

Hero Survivors is an intense, action-packed survival game where players face overwhelming hordes of monsters. The core challenge is to survive as long as possible by strategically choosing powerful, auto-firing abilities and creating devastating skill combinations to clear the screen of enemies.

Project Info

  • Role: Game Developer & Designer
  • Team Size: 5-10
  • Time frame: 2023
  • Engine: Unity

Development Deep Dive

The mission for Heroes Survivor was to create a compelling and visually satisfying title within the popular "bullet heaven" survival genre, focusing on high replayability and a strong sense of power progression. My role was to design the core combat experience and ensure the game could technically handle the on-screen chaos inherent to the genre.

From a design perspective, I focused on creating unique character skills that would be effective and satisfying in an auto-firing combat system. A significant part of my responsibility was the design and balancing of the monster wave system; I meticulously scripted the timing, composition, and intensity of enemy swarms to build a dynamic difficulty curve that was both challenging and fair. I also designed and fine-tuned the behaviors for all bosses to make them memorable encounters that tested the player's build and skills.

The primary technical hurdle for this genre is maintaining smooth performance while rendering hundreds of independent enemies and projectiles simultaneously. My solution was to implement a comprehensive optimization strategy centered around object pooling for not just VFX, but also for enemies and loot drops. I also optimized the AI calculation loop to reduce its performance cost when scaled up to hundreds of agents. This technical foundation was critical to delivering a fluid, lag-free experience, even during the most chaotic late-game moments.

My Contributions

My contributions involved designing the core combat feel and implementing the systems required to bring it to life.

As a Game Developer:
  • Monster Wave & Spawning System: Developed the system responsible for managing and spawning massive waves of monsters, scripting their timing and patterns to control the flow of gameplay.
  • Boss Behavior Scripting: Wrote the C# scripts to implement the unique AI, attack patterns, and special abilities for all in-game bosses.
  • Character Skill Implementation: Programmed the functionality for all unique character skills, translating design concepts into functional in-game mechanics.
  • Performance Optimization: Proactively identified and solved performance bottlenecks related to rendering a high number of on-screen enemies and effects, ensuring the game ran smoothly.
As a Game Designer:
  • Unique Skill Design: Conceptualized and designed a wide array of character skills tailored for the "survivor" genre, focusing on synergies and satisfying visual impact.
  • VFX Design & Creation: Designed and created captivating VFX for all character abilities and enemy effects, providing clear visual feedback to the player during chaotic combat.
  • Level & Encounter Balancing: Balanced the difficulty of chapter levels by fine-tuning monster stats, spawn rates, and boss encounters to create an engaging and replayable experience.

Archer Hunter

About

Archer Hunter is an addictive, action-packed adventure game where you slay hordes of enemies with an amazing set of skills and weapons. Begin as a newbie archer, then build your skills and abilities to become the Master.

Project Info

  • Role: Game Developer & Designer
  • Team Size: 5-10
  • Time frame: 2022
  • Engine: Unity

Development Deep Dive

As the original flagship title, the mission for Archer Hunter was to create a highly engaging and scalable roguelike experience from the ground up, built around an addictive core combat loop. My role was foundational, involving the architecture of core gameplay systems and establishing the technical groundwork to ensure a smooth, high-quality player experience.

From a design perspective, I was responsible for architecting the game’s core systems. This began with designing a versatile character skill system that allowed for dynamic, procedurally-generated builds in each run. I developed the initial AI for a wide range of enemies, creating the fundamental behavior patterns that defined the game's challenge. Furthermore, I conceptualized the Player vs. Player (PVP) mode and the In-App Purchase (IAP) model, laying the groundwork for the game's long-term engagement and monetization strategies.

Technically, our primary goal was to deliver high-fidelity visuals and effects on a wide range of mobile devices without compromising performance. The visually-intensive VFX for character skills presented a significant performance hurdle. To solve this proactively, I engineered a robust object pooling system as a core part of our architecture. Instead of constantly creating and destroying costly particle effects, this system recycled them efficiently. This foundational decision ensured we could maintain a stable 60 FPS during heavy combat, establishing a "performance-by-design" philosophy for the project from day one.

My Contributions

As a hybrid developer and designer, I was responsible for architecting the game's foundational systems and shaping the initial player experience.

As a Game Developer:
  • Core Systems Architecture: Engineered the foundational gameplay systems, including the character controller and combat loop, ensuring a scalable and maintainable codebase from the start.
  • VFX Implementation: Created and integrated a library of custom, performance-friendly Visual Effects (VFX) for all initial character skills using Unity's particle system.
  • Feature Prototyping: Developed the initial prototypes for key game features, including the Player vs. Player (PVP) mode and the In-App Purchase (IAP) framework, to validate their technical feasibility.
As a Game Designer:
  • Initial Concept & GDD: Played a key role in architecting the game's vision and documenting it in the initial Game Design Document (GDD).
  • Foundational Skill System: Designed the initial versatile character skill system, focusing on creating dynamic, procedurally-generated builds for each run.
  • Fundamental AI Design: Developed the fundamental behavior patterns for a wide range of enemies, establishing the core challenge and feel of the game's combat.
